CONQUEST ADVISORS

Conquest Advisors LTD is a privately owned multi strategy real estate advisor providing its clientele with 360 degree coverage ranging from transactions to generational investments. Its services primarily focus on advisory and private equity. Its clientele includes Family Offices, Developers, Owners, and Opportunity Funds.

The firm's advisory activities have spanned both commercial and residential transactions including new development, building conversions, hotel and office buildings, apartment purchases, and commercial leasing.

Private equity advisory engagements range from sourcing lower risk cash flowing opportunities on behalf of family offices to locating LP capital for sponsors on value-add investments. The firm's members have been associated with REBNY, CREFC and ULI. Conquest's deals have been featured in major news outlets including the Wall Street Journal, NY Times and the NY Post.
