Conquest Advisors LTD is a privately owned real estate advisor primarily focused on brokerage, development and private equity. It provides its services to Individuals, Family Offices, Developers, Owners, and Opportunity Funds.

The firm’s brokerage activities have spanned both commercial and residential transactions including new development, building conversions, hotel and office buildings, apartment purchases, and commercial leasing. The firm has participated in a number of development activities including multi-family buildings and apartment renovations.

The firm also provides private equity services. These services range from sourcing LP capital, for Sponsors to advisory engagements targeting opportunistic and value added investments. The firm’s members have been associated with REBNY, CREFC and ULI. The firms deals have been featured in major news outlets including the Wall Street Journal, NY Times, NY Post.
